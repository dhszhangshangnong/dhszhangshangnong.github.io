Superman's Space

You got to get to know me.

My name is Kal-El and I was born on the planet Krypton, before being rocketed to Earth as an infant by my scientist father Jor-El, moments before Krypton's destruction.

I was discovered and adopted by a Kansas farm couple, raised as Clark Kent, and imbued with a strong moral compass.

Very early on I started to display various superhuman abilities, which, upon reaching maturity, I resolved to use for the benefit of humanity through a secret "Superman" identity.

Where you'll find me.

I reside and operate in the American city of Metropolis as a journalist for the Daily Planet.
